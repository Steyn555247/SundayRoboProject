Clean wheel sensor

Debris on the right front wheel sensor is reducing Memo's navigation accuracy. A quick clean takes about 2 minutes and no tools are needed.

1
Make sure Memo is parked
Tap "Stop" if Memo is mid-task. Wait for the status light to turn solid white before continuing.
2
Locate the right front wheel
Face Memo from the front. The right front wheel is on your left. The sensor is the small dark lens on the inner edge of the wheel housing.
The sensor is a small circular lens about 8mm wide, flush with the wheel casing.
3
Wipe with a soft dry cloth
Use a microfibre cloth or a dry cotton pad. Gently wipe the lens in a circular motion 3–4 times. No liquids needed.
Avoid paper towels — they can leave lint on the sensor.
4
Restart Memo's navigation
Tap the button below to confirm the clean is done. Memo will run a quick sensor check and resume normal operation.
Never use water, alcohol, or cleaning sprays near the sensors. The silicone shell can be wiped with household cleaners, but sensor lenses should only be dry-cleaned.
